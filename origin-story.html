STORY

Planet Bao is now led by Blop, a powerful leader who dominate the entire planet. Like what usually happens in politics, Blop did a lot of corruption and there is a big gap of social inequality in the planet. However, the society has no power to fight back Blop because he has a superpower where he can change himself to become very big and destroy everything that he wants.

At the same time, there is a young adult called Olax who is the most intelligent person in the planet. Olax born in a decent family but his parents always teach him to always be honest and to defend the truth. However, his parents also give him freedom to explore about himself more. Therefore, Olax become a very trusted person who always find the truth no matter how hard it is.

In the next 3 months, planet Bao will hold an election for the new leader. Olax has determined to nominate himself in this election to compete with Blop. Therefore, Olax is campaigning to get peoples attention to vote for him.
